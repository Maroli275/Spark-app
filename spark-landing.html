FAQ

Questions,
answered.

What exactly is a "Spark"? +
A Spark is a daily 5–15 minute challenge designed to get you off passive scrolling and into doing something real. They range from creative (draw a self-portrait from memory) to physical (try box breathing) to intellectual (solve a lateral thinking puzzle) to culinary (open-fridge experiment). Each one is hand-crafted to be genuinely interesting — not just a checkbox.
Do I need any equipment or skills? +
Zero. Every challenge is designed to be done with whatever you have around you — a pen and paper, your fridge, a quiet room, your own voice. No gym, no art supplies, no musical ability required. The point is to lower the barrier to doing something, not raise it.
What if I miss a day? +
Free users lose their streak (but keep all their XP and completed Sparks). Pro users get a Streak Shield — two per month — that lets you skip a day without penalty. We're also exploring a "grace period" feature. Life happens. Spark is about building a habit, not punishing you for being human.
How does the AI reaction work? +
After completing a challenge, you can optionally write a quick reaction — what happened, what surprised you, what you made. Our AI (powered by Claude) reads your specific response and writes back: a warm, two-sentence message that acknowledges what YOU experienced and adds one surprising related fact or insight. It never gives a generic "great job!" It actually responds to what you wrote.
How is this different from Duolingo? +
Duolingo teaches you one specific skill (language). Spark introduces you to a rotating range of human experiences — creativity, movement, thinking, making, wondering. You're not building toward a certificate or a test. You're building a more interesting, more awake version of your daily life. It's also much less guilt-trip-y.
Can I cancel Pro anytime? +
Yes, anytime. No dark patterns, no cancellation interviews, no hidden fees. Cancel in one tap from your profile settings. If you cancel mid-period, you keep access until the end of your billing cycle.
